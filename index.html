☕ Café Rewards Report ☕

This is another challenge from Maven Analytics that uses a data base simulating the behavior of Cafe Rewards members over a 30-day period, including Maven Analytics (https://mavenanalytics.io) promotional offers.

Key objective for the challenge:

After running a test by sending different combinations of promotional offers to existing rewards members, we now have a 30-day data base of strategy for future promotional messaging & targeting.

Objective: the task is to identify key customer segments and develop a data-driven strategy for future promotional messaging & targeting.

Find a persona target for a new marketing promotion/campaign after a previous promotion test during 30 days.

Data analysis:

With python and power query I first did data preparation (extraction, cleaning and formatting);

Next steps was data modeling and metrics in order to find questions/answers that the data analysis could give (about the events, customer segments and offers).

Assumptions:

  • 1. There seem to be some errors with customers who are 118 years old with no gender and no income (removed from the analysis – despite having transactions – to focus on the target persona);
  • 2. The events per se don't actually tell me anything about the channel, there's nothing specifying if it was e-mail, mobile, social media, or all of events
  • 3. In theory, the person receives, sees and completes the offer but there are case/events where the person has received and completed the events
